About Jumbo’s

Since 1978, Serving Tulsa with Love

Jumbo’s Hamburgers is a local favorite, known for generous portions, fresh ingredients, and unbeatable prices. We started with one grill and a dream — to feed our neighbors the best burgers in town. Decades later, we’ve expanded our menu to include Mediterranean specialties, kids meals, and hearty dinners.

Our mission is simple: feed people like family. We keep it fresh, keep it friendly, and keep it real.
