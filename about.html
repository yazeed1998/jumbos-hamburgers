Our Story

Proudly serving Tulsa since 1978

From Small Beginnings

Jumbo’s Hamburgers began as a family-run stand in Tulsa, capturing hearts with our signature burgers and homemade sauces. Over the decades, we’ve stayed true to our roots—fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Today, you’ll still find the same passion in every bite: handcrafted patties, savory Mediterranean specialties, and our all‑time favorite chili, simmered to perfection every morning. Our goal? To make every visit feel like coming home.
