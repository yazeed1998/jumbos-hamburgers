About Jumbo’s

Since 1978, Jumbo's Hamburgers has been a staple in Tulsa, serving up mouthwatering burgers, hearty chili, and Mediterranean specialties. We’re proud to be locally owned and operated, offering high-quality meals with that homemade taste and unbeatable value.

Our legacy is built on family traditions, loyal customers, and a commitment to real food with real flavor. Whether you're here for a gyro burger or a classic cheeseburger, Jumbo’s delivers a satisfying experience every time.
